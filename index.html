About Clarity

Clarity was made in 2025, by a student in Hungary. Her name was Polokalap.
She bought a 1 year subscription to a Video Editing software by an unnamed company,
and when she tried to cancel it, she had to pay a fee of 90 euros.
After that, she got really-really angry, and started making Clarity.
